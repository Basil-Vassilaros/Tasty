Our History

Our founder, John Van De Merwe, was born and bred here in South Africa to caterers Peter and Sarah Van De Merwe. During his younger years, he always admired his parents and their cooking, the way their customers always smiled after eating a meal. This inspired his dream to serve a smile to people with the taste of his food.

Following his dream, he started working as a waiter at a small cafe in Johanesberg, Derick's Coffee. There he worked his way up, learning as much as he could and when the owner moved to America, where his children lived - giving the cafe to John for all his hard work.

Now, 10 years later, Tasty has become a becon of good food and comfort, one that aims to bring smiles the same way John's parents did.

  • Our Timeline

  • 1989

    John Van De Merwe is born to Peter & Sarah Van De Merwe.

  • 1995

    John at the age of 16 starts working part-time as a waiter at Derick's Coffee.

  • 1997

    John matriculates and starts to work full-time at Derick's Coffee.

  • 2002

    John saves up enough money to study business management and cooking.

  • 2005

    John graduates and returns to Derick's Coffee to find it without a cook and not much customers.

  • 2007

    Wth John as the new cook, Derick's Coffee took off with a new popularity.

  • 2012

    Derick moves to America, transfering ownership of the cafe to John - who renames the cafe to Tasty.

  • 2015

    Tasty begins a reformation into a cafe-resteraunt.

  • 2018

    Tasty is awarded South Africa's Best Coffee.

  • 2020

    Tasty closes due to the pandemic.

  • 2021

    Tasty has its grand re-opening and becomes even more popular than before.
